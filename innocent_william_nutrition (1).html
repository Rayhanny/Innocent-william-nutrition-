About the Project

The Innocent William Food & Nutrition Project aims to educate communities and individuals about the importance of balanced diets, food safety, and proper nutrition. Our goal is to empower people to live healthier lives by making informed food choices.

Nutritional Facts

  • Carbohydrates are the body's main energy source.
  • Proteins help build and repair body tissues.
  • Fats provide energy and help absorb vitamins.
  • Vitamins and minerals are essential for various bodily functions.
  • Water is vital for digestion, absorption, and overall hydration.

Healthy Recipes

  • Fruit Smoothie: Blend bananas, strawberries, milk, and honey.
  • Veggie Stir-fry: Mix broccoli, carrots, bell peppers with olive oil and soy sauce.
  • Oats & Nuts: A nutritious breakfast of oats, almonds, and raisins.

Food Education

Learning about food labels, understanding food groups, and knowing how to safely prepare meals are key parts of food education. We provide workshops, handouts, and online resources to help our audience make smarter decisions around food and health.
